# SIHM Dimapur CMS Admin Guide

## Daily Content Workflow

1. Log in to Strapi Admin.
2. Update content in the relevant collection.
3. Upload images, PDFs, and videos through the Media Library.
4. Fill SEO fields before publishing.
5. Use Draft first, then Publish when approved.

## Recommended Collections

- Hero Banners: homepage cinematic slider content.
- Courses: course cards, eligibility, fees, curriculum, brochure PDFs.
- Faculty: faculty cards, profiles, achievements.
- News & Events: campus news, circulars, admission notices, examination updates.
- Notices: official circulars and downloadable attachments.
- Recruiters: placement logo carousel.
- Placements: student placement outcomes.
- Gallery Items: campus photos and videos.
- Testimonials: student, alumni, and mentor quotes.
- Alumni Stories: long-form success stories.
- FAQs: admissions and course questions.

## Media Guidance

Use landscape images at 1600px wide for hero and section banners. Use compressed JPG/WebP wherever possible. Add descriptive alt text in the media name or caption.

## SEO Guidance

Every public page/content entry should have a clear meta title, description, and canonical URL. Use concise titles such as `B.Sc. HHA Course | SIHM Dimapur`.
